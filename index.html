Trans youth in Los Angeles and all over the world have been continuously disadvantaged and ignored. We aim to disrupt this silence in a discreet and safe manner



According to the Journal of Hate Studies, "hate crimes against transgender and gender non-conforming individuals have increased 587% between 2013 and 2019" which is likely a fraction of the issue due to reporting inaccuracies and underreporting.

About Us

We get it, getting the proper information and resources when you are part of the lgbtq+ community is hard; It can even feel impossible.


We are a small group of queer students who have experienced oppression in white suburbia, and are focused on providing a curation of different resources for trans and non-binary youth.
